## Improve Initial Onboarding Experience

The initial blank screen does not provide any guidance or value to the user. This space can be utilized more effectively by adding a simple onboarding element, such as:

- A short demo video, or
- Brief instructions on how to use the product

This would help users understand the product quickly and reduce initial friction.

Alternatively, the input textbox could be positioned centrally (similar to chat-based interfaces like ChatGPT) to make the interaction more intuitive and focused.
